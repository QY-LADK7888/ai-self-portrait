# Vision

The full picture, beyond Phase 1.

---

## What we're building

Imagine **MBTI Premium**, but:

- **Conversational** instead of static questionnaire
- **Cross-domain inference** instead of fixed personality categories
- **10 dimensions** of self-knowledge instead of 4 letters
- Output: a *life advisor for yourself*, not a personality label

The Skill (when complete) produces a personalized analysis as deep as a 14-hour 1-on-1 with a sharp coach who happens to know you cold.

---

## The 10 dimensions

When the full Skill ships (Phase 3 vision), it outputs analysis across:

1. **Who you are** — character, patterns, contradictions you don't see
2. **Your strengths** — what you actually do better than 90% in your context
3. **Your weaknesses** — including blind spots you keep walking into
4. **Skills you have** — actually-paid-for vs claimed-but-untested
5. **Skills you should learn** — based on direction + current gap, not generic Top-10 lists
6. **Career direction** — what kind of work fits you specifically, with reasoning
7. **Your future** — concrete picture, not vague "be happy" or "be successful"
8. **Your development path** — 90-day / 1-year / 5-year specific actions
9. **Money & value philosophy** — what you actually believe vs what you say you believe
10. **Life philosophy** — meaning, ethics, fears, what you want your existence to add up to

Phase 1 today covers dimensions 1-3 (partial). Phase 2 adds 4-6. Phase 3 adds 7-10.

## Phase roadmap

### Phase 1 — Self-Portrait Foundation · ✅ Shipped today

**What you get**: 2 markdown files
- `<nickname>-introduction.md` — inferred portrait, not restated facts
- `<nickname>-style.md` — how you want AI to collaborate with you

**Dimensions covered**: 1 (who you are), partial 2-3 (strengths / weaknesses surfaced as patterns)

**Why ship now**: A foundation users can actually use today beats a "perfect" version that ships in 6 months. Real users → real feedback → Phase 2 design adjusts → ship Phase 2.

---

### Phase 2 — Path Analysis · 🔜 2-4 weeks

**Adds**:
- Skill assessment with confidence levels (verified vs claimed)
- Career direction matching (jobs / paths that fit your specific stack, with reasoning)
- Resource recommendations (books / courses / tools, personalized to your direction — not generic Top-10 lists)
- Pre-mortems for each candidate path (how this path could fail for *you*)

**Dimensions covered**: 4 (skills you have), 5 (skills to learn), 6 (career direction)

**Output adds**: `<nickname>-path-analysis.md`

---

### Phase 3 — Living Life Plan · 🔜 2-3 months

**Adds**:
- 90-day / 1-year / 5-year concrete action plans, not vague aspirations
- Money / value / life philosophy surfacing through scenario-based questioning
- Monthly re-interview workflow — your portrait stays current as you grow
- Decision support — when a major life decision shows up, the Skill helps you frame it against your own values, not generic advice

**Dimensions covered**: 7 (future), 8 (development path), 9 (money / values), 10 (life philosophy)

**Output adds**: `<nickname>-life-plan.md`, `<nickname>-decision-frame.md`

## Why phased shipping

I could build all 10 dimensions and ship in 3 months. But:

- Building blind without user feedback → high risk Phase 2/3 misses real needs
- "Perfect" version too often becomes "never shipped" version
- One-person company velocity loves narrow scope; broad scope kills momentum

So: Phase 1 ships today. Real users → real feedback → Phase 2 spec evolves accordingly.

Public progress + real data every week on X.

---

## Why this matters

99% of people use AI as if it knows them. It doesn't.

Most prompts try to fix this by adding context inline. But you can only type so much each time. And you forget what you already explained.

Once your context lives in a portable file, every AI conversation starts deeper. Every piece of advice gets sharper. Every decision the AI helps you think through is grounded in **who you actually are**, not who AI imagined.

That's the bet. We'll see if it works.

## 中文版

### 10 个维度

1. 你是什么样的人 — 性格 / pattern / 你看不到的矛盾
2. 你的优势 — 你在你的 context 里做得比 90% 人好的事
3. 你的缺点 — 包括你反复撞的 blind spot
4. 你拥有的技能 — 真的被人付钱过 vs 自称会
5. 你适合学的技能 — 基于方向 + 当前 gap, 不是 generic Top-10
6. 你适合的工作方向 — 不是泛泛, 是基于你的 stack 推断
7. 你的未来 — 具体画面, 不是"开心""成功"这种空话
8. 你的发展路径 — 90 天 / 1 年 / 5 年具体行动
9. 金钱观 / 价值观 — 你**实际**信什么 vs 你**说**你信什么
10. 人生观 — 意义 / 伦理 / 恐惧 / 你想让你的存在加起来等于什么
